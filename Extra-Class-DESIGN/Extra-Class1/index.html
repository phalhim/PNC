    What is a domestic dog?

    The term “domestic dog” refers to any of several hundred breeds of dog in the world today. While these animals vary drastically in appearance, every dog—from the Chihuahua to the Great Dane—is a member of the same species, Canis familiaris.

      ANIMAL SREFERENCE
  • COMMON NAME: Domestic dogs
  • SCIENTIFIC NAME: Canis familiaris
  • TYPE: Mammals
  • DIET: Omnivore
  • GROUP NAME: Pack
  • WEIGHT: Three to 250 pounds
